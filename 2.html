Beach House
Lincoln City, Oregon

Situated just two hundred feet from the crashing surf, the property is a 50'x100' infill lot in an older neighborhood. Sloped site; ocean views improving with height. A smaller building footprint w/ three stories got us high enough to capture panoramic views for the top floor - living, dining, kitchen, reading, and half bath. Middle and lower floors house 4 great bdrms, each with windows on two sides (3 of 4 opening onto decks). Emphasis on very efficient floor plan while providing views and light for almost every space.
