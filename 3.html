New Home
Suburban Forested acreage
Portland, Oregon

New home built on 2-1/2 acres of largely forested land, 10 minutes from downtown Portland, Oregon. Fits into an existing neighborhood of eclectic mix of 1950s-80s homes. Wanted large 3,500sf house, yet a modest presence. Modern planning with traditional touches. Passive solar orientation features a 50' long by 12' wide porch, protecting the living space from hot summer sun, but welcoming the low winter sun.
